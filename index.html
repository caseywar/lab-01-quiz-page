About Me

My name is Casey Warren. I am a fun, outgoing person who always loves a new adventure. I am multifacited in that I can be as hype as a 21 year old college kid or as chill as a 87 year old woman. I like to exist in both states equally. In the following paragraphs I will list facts about myself that you better remember, there will be a quiz.

I am 27 years old and live in Kalamazoo, MI. I am originally from a small town called Middleville, but I have lived in Kalamazoo for the last 10 years. Kalamazoo has a thriving music and art scene. Pre-covid, there would be concerts downtown or house shows every weekend. Most people in our community strive to create a safe space for everyone. It is a truly wonderful and fun place!

One of my favorite pastimes is going camping. There is an amazing spot about 2 hours from where I live, we call it 'The Spot'. The Spot is amazing because it is all rustic camping, you can choose to camp by your car or hike in and camp on the cliff or in the sand dunes. You can also have fires right on the shore of Lake Michigan. It is not monitored by DNR heavily so theres baiscally no rules, its great! this summer I went camping on:
  • June 27, 2020
  • July 20, 2020
  • August 7, 2020
  • August 23, 2020
  • Septemmber 13, 2020

It was a succesful summer of camping!
